Remarks

Lives in the Core assembly so the conversion is unit-tested without a Godot runtime. The adapter calls into these helpers instead of hand-rolling the math in two places.

Anything below MinAudibleLinear is treated as full silence and clamped to SilenceDb, which keeps log10(0) from blowing up and matches the intuitive "slider all the way down = muted" behaviour.
