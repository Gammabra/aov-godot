Remarks

Aligned with BattleInputSystem, which exposes five hot-keys (battle_select_skill1..battle_select_skill5), the loadout has exactly MaxActiveSlots slots.

A skill can only occupy a slot if the owner's level meets its unlock requirement (IsSkillUnlocked(string, int)). Passive skills are NOT placed here — they auto-apply once unlocked and are tracked on CharacterProfile.
