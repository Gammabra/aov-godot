Remarks

The actual targeting bypass is implemented by the AI controllers and the player input layer: when a unit has this effect, both BasicEnemyAi and BattleHud consult UnitSystem.HasEffect<T> and pick a random valid target instead of the chosen one.

Non-stackable, purifiable. Per the doc, "Confusion: attacks at random (enemy or ally) — non stackable, purifiable".
