Remarks

Godot calls a node's _Ready at a moment that is not synchronised with the _Ready of nodes added to the tree on the same frame. So when a widget runs its _Ready, Instance may not yet be populated (the bus's _Ready is still queued).

WhenReadyAsync(Node, Action<BattleEventBus>) polls one frame at a time until the bus is alive, then lets the caller perform its subscription. This is the canonical way for HUD widgets to attach themselves to the bus from inside their own _Ready.
