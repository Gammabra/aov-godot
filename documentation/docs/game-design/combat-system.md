# ⚔️ Combat System

The combat system in **Ashes of Velsingrad** is tactical and strategic, emphasizing positioning, terrain usage, and careful resource management.

## Core Mechanics

### Turn-Based Tactical Combat

Combat occurs on a grid-based battlefield where positioning is crucial:

- **Initiative System**: Determines turn order based on speed and tactical awareness
- **Action Points**: Each unit has limited actions per turn (move, attack, cast spell, use item)
- **Opportunity Attacks**: Moving past enemies triggers counterattacks
- **Flanking**: Attacking from sides or behind provides damage bonuses

### Terrain Effects

The battlefield is more than just decoration:

| Terrain Type | Effect | Strategic Use |
|--------------|--------|---------------|
| **High Ground** | +15% accuracy, +10% damage | Archer positioning |
| **Cover** | +25% dodge chance | Protection from ranged |
| **Mud/Water** | -50% movement speed | Area denial |
| **Fire** | 5 damage per turn | Damage over time zones |
| **Ice** | Slip chance on movement | Movement control |

### Weather System

Environmental conditions affect combat:

- **Rain**: +20% lightning damage, -10% fire damage
- **Fog**: -30% ranged accuracy, +stealth effectiveness
- **Snow**: -1 movement per turn, fire spells melt snow
- **Sandstorm**: -50% visibility, earth spells more effective

## Unit Types and Roles

### The Iron Crows

Each member of your mercenary company has distinct roles:

#### **Kaelen Voss** (Commander)
- **Role**: Versatile leader with dark magic
- **Abilities**: Command skills, curse magic, weapon mastery

- **Abilities**: High HP, armor, weapon skills

#### **Rangers**

- **Abilities**: Bow mastery, tracking, survival skills

#### **Mages**
- **Role**: Elemental magic and battlefield control
- **Abilities**: Spell casting, elemental mastery, arcane knowledge
- **Specializations**: Elementalist (damage), Controller (battlefield), Healer (support)

#### **Rogues**

- **Abilities**: Stealth, lockpicking, backstab, poison
- **Specializations**: Assassin (damage), Infiltrator (stealth), Saboteur (utility)

## Combat Mechanics

### Action Economy

Each unit's turn consists of:

1. **Movement Phase**: Move up to movement range
2. **Action Phase**: Choose one primary action:
   - **Attack**: Weapon or spell attack
   - **Full Defense**: +50% dodge until next turn
   - **Charge**: Move extra distance with attack bonus
   - **Prepare**: Ready an action for enemy movement
3. **Free Actions**: Unlimited (speak, drop item, activate passive abilities)

### Damage and Health

#### Health System
- **Hit Points (HP)**: Current health
- **Stamina**: Used for special abilities
- **Morale**: Affects accuracy and damage

#### Damage Types
- **Physical**: Reduced by armor
- **Elemental**: Affected by resistances
- **Curse**: Ignores most defenses
- **True**: Cannot be reduced

#### Status Effects
- **Bleeding**: Damage over time
- **Burning**: Fire damage each turn
- **Frozen**: Cannot act for one turn
- **Cursed**: Various negative effects
- **Blessed**: Various positive effects

### Magic in Combat

Magic is powerful but comes with risks:

#### Spell Schools
1. **Fire**: High damage, area effects, burning
2. **Water**: Healing, freezing, battlefield control
3. **Earth**: Defense, terrain manipulation, crushing
4. **Air**: Lightning, speed, battlefield mobility
5. **Light**: Healing, buffs, curse removal
6. **Darkness**: Debuffs, lifesteal, fear effects

#### Corruption System
Using dark magic increases Kaelen's corruption:

- **0-25%**: No penalties
- **26-50%**: -10% light magic effectiveness
- **51-75%**: Random dark magic surges
- **76-100%**: Risk of losing control

## Advanced Tactics

### Formation Combat

Positioning your units effectively:

- **Phalanx**: Shields front, spears behind
- **Archer Screen**: Ranged behind melee protection
- **Pincer**: Flank enemies from multiple sides
- **Retreat Formation**: Organized withdrawal

### Environmental Interaction

- **Destructible Terrain**: Break walls, collapse ceilings
- **Interactive Objects**: Push rocks, activate switches
- **Elevation Changes**: Use stairs, ramps, cliffs
- **Spell Combinations**: Freeze water, ignite oil, electrify metal

### Morale and Leadership

#### Morale Effects
- **High Morale**: +10% accuracy, +5% damage
- **Broken Morale**: -20% accuracy, flee chance
- **Inspiring Presence**: Kaelen's leadership affects nearby allies

#### Leadership Abilities
- **Rally**: Restore morale to nearby units
- **Tactical Strike**: Coordinate multi-unit attacks
- **Retreat**: Organized withdrawal with minimal losses
- **Last Stand**: Boost damage when outnumbered

## Enemy AI

### AI Behavior Types

#### **Aggressive**: Rush forward, prioritize damage
#### **Defensive**: Hold position, protect objectives
#### **Tactical**: Use terrain, focus fire, retreat when wounded
#### **Berserker**: Ignore self-preservation, maximum aggression
#### **Support**: Stay back, buff allies, heal wounded

### Faction Combat Styles

#### **Imperial Soldiers**
- **Tactics**: Formation fighting, discipline
- **Strengths**: Armor, coordination, ranged support
- **Weaknesses**: Inflexible, vulnerable to magic

#### **Inquisition Forces**
- **Tactics**: Anti-magic focus, holy weapons
- **Strengths**: Curse resistance, healing, undead immunity
- **Weaknesses**: Vulnerable to overwhelming force

#### **Corrupted Enemies**
- **Tactics**: Unpredictable, fear effects, regeneration
- **Strengths**: High damage, status effects, numbers
- **Weaknesses**: Light magic, low organization

#### **Wild Creatures**
- **Tactics**: Pack hunting, environmental advantages
- **Strengths**: Speed, natural weapons, terrain knowledge
- **Weaknesses**: Low intelligence, predictable patterns

## Tips for Success

### General Strategy
1. **Control the high ground** whenever possible
2. **Use terrain** to your advantage
3. **Focus fire** on priority targets
4. **Protect your wounded** - retreat is sometimes necessary
5. **Manage resources** - don't waste mana or stamina

### Combat Preparation
- **Scout ahead** when possible
- **Choose appropriate equipment** for expected enemies
- **Plan spell usage** to minimize corruption
- **Position units** according to their strengths

### Emergency Tactics
- **Fighting retreat** when overwhelmed
- **Last stand formations** when surrounded
- **Sacrifice plays** to save the majority
- **Environmental escapes** using spells or terrain
