# ✨ Magic System

The magic system in **Ashes of Velsingrad** is designed to be both a source of power and a dangerous temptation. It blends classical elemental magic with the dark and corrupting forces of the Curse, forcing players to carefully weigh their choices.

---

## 🌌 Core Principles of Magic

### Magic as a Double-Edged Sword
Magic in Velsingrad is not just a tool; it is a force tied to the **Curse**, a malevolent energy that corrupts the land and its people. Every spell cast by Kaelen and certain companions may come at the cost of corruption, sanity, or loyalty.

### Schools of Magic
There are **six primary schools** of magic, each with its own strengths, weaknesses, and thematic style:

1. **Fire** – Destruction, area-of-effect damage, and burning status.
2. **Water** – Healing, freezing, and battlefield control.
3. **Earth** – Defense, terrain manipulation, and crowd control.
4. **Air** – Lightning, mobility, and precision.
5. **Light** – Protection, cleansing curses, and buffs.
6. **Darkness** – Debuffs, fear, lifesteal, and summoning.

---

## 🔮 Spellcasting Mechanics

### Mana and Stamina
- **Mana (MP):** Consumed by most magical spells.
- **Stamina (SP):** Required for hybrid abilities that mix physical and magical effects.
- **Corruption Points (CP):** Accumulate when using dark or cursed spells. At high CP, Kaelen risks triggering **Corruption Surges** (uncontrollable magical outbursts).

### Spell Levels
Each school of magic is divided into **three tiers**:
- **Apprentice** – Basic spells (e.g., Firebolt, Minor Heal).
- **Adept** – Intermediate control spells (e.g., Fire Wall, Ice Prison).
- **Master** – Devastating ultimate abilities (e.g., Meteor Storm, Blackened Sun).

### Casting Phases
Casting a spell requires:
1. **Targeting Phase:** Selecting a target or area.
2. **Channeling Phase:** Some spells require multiple turns to charge.
3. **Execution Phase:** The spell is unleashed, with potential side effects if interrupted.

---

## 🕯️ Corruption System

### Corruption Thresholds
Using **Darkness** magic or invoking the **Curse** increases Kaelen’s corruption level:
- **0-25%:** No penalties.
- **26-50%:** Light magic effectiveness decreases by 10%.
- **51-75%:** Random dark magic surges (unintended effects).
- **76-100%:** Kaelen risks **Losing Control**, triggering a "Feral Corruption State" where he attacks allies and enemies indiscriminately.

### Cleansing Corruption
- **Light Magic:** Certain spells cleanse corruption.
- **Sanctuaries:** Special locations can purify the soul at a narrative cost.
- **Sacrifices:** Offering life energy (HP) or morale to lower corruption.

## 🧩 Elemental Interactions

Magic can interact dynamically with the environment and other spells:

| Interaction | Effect |
|-------------|--------|
| **Fire + Oil** | Ignites the area, dealing extra burning damage. |
| **Water + Lightning** | Electrifies water, damaging all units standing in it. |
| **Ice + Fire** | Melts ice terrain, creating mud that slows movement. |
| **Earth + Air** | Creates dust storms, reducing visibility. |

## 📜 Example Spells

### Fire School
- **Firebolt (Apprentice):** Single-target fire damage.
- **Flame Surge (Adept):** Creates a wall of fire that burns for 3 turns.
- **Meteor Storm (Master):** Calls down meteors over a wide area.

### Water School
- **Minor Heal (Apprentice):** Restores a small amount of HP.
- **Ice Prison (Adept):** Freezes enemies for 1 turn.
- **Tidal Wave (Master):** Pushes enemies back and deals water damage.

### Darkness School
- **Drain Life (Apprentice):** Steals HP from a target.
- **Fear Wave (Adept):** Reduces enemy morale, causing panic.
- **Blackened Sun (Master):** Creates an eclipse, dealing AoE curse damage and reducing accuracy.

---

## 🧙 Character Magic Specializations

### Kaelen Voss
Kaelen's magic is heavily influenced by the Curse:
- Can access **all schools**, but dark magic grows stronger as corruption rises.
- At high corruption, unique "forbidden" spells unlock.

### Companion Mages
Each mage companion specializes in one or two schools:
- **Lyra, the Frost-Seer:** Water + Air control.
- **Elandor, the Ashbinder:** Fire + Darkness.
- **Seraphine, the Lightbearer:** Light + Earth healing/support.

---

## 🎭 Role of Magic in the Narrative

Magic is deeply tied to the **storytelling and moral choices**:
- Excessive use of dark magic affects **companions’ loyalty** (some may leave or betray Kaelen).
- Entire factions react to how much Kaelen relies on the Curse (e.g., **Inquisition hunts him more aggressively**).
- Special **narrative events** trigger when mastering certain schools, unlocking unique endings.

---

## 🏆 Advanced Magic Strategies

- **Elemental Chains:** Combine spells across schools for devastating effects (e.g., freeze enemies with water, then shatter them with earth).
- **Environmental Control:** Alter terrain to gain tactical advantages.
- **Sacrificial Power:** Channel corruption to cast ultimate spells earlier, but risk losing control.
