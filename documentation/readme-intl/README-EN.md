# 🏹 **Ashes Of Velsingrad**

*An RPG of Betrayal, Power, and Redemption*

---

## 🌍 **1146 After the Fall**

Velsingrad is in ruins, ravaged by decades of war with the Empire of Solmire.
Just as peace seemed possible, the darkness emerged:

- **The Plague** – A black fever that consumed nobles and peasants alike in days.
- **The Inquisition** – The Order of the Broken Dawn blamed sorcery and launched a brutal purge.
- **The Curse** – A dark force awakened beneath the land, stirring in the veins of the damned.

You are **Kaelen Voss**, commander of the once-feared **Iron Crows**, betrayed and left for dead.
A dark power now courses through your veins — a power feared by the Inquisition and coveted by sorcerers.

👉 Will you seek **Vengeance** or **Redemption** — or will you dominate all that stands before you?

---

## 🎯 **Core Gameplay Loop**

The core experience is built around exploration, tactical combat, and moral choice:

1. **🌲 Exploration & Survival**
   - Travel through war-torn cities, corrupted villages, and haunted ruins.
   - Manage resources (food, medicine, equipment) to keep your company alive.
   - Interact with NPCs — survivors, mercenaries, inquisitors, and hidden sorcerers.

2. **📜 The Challenge Board**
   - Available in taverns and refugee camps.
   - Complete contracts (assassination, sabotage, rescue).
   - Gain reputation and resources — or suffer the consequences of failure.

3. **⚔️ Tactical Combat**
   - **Turn-based or real-time** (player’s choice).
   - Master terrain, unit positioning, and synergy between abilities.
   - Kaelen’s curse grants dark powers — but at what cost?

4. **🏕️ Company Management**
   - Reunite and recruit the **Iron Crows**.
   - Manage morale and loyalty.
   - Upgrade your camp and unlock new skills.

5. **🔥 Power Progression**
   - Develop Kaelen’s cursed abilities — control them or embrace the darkness.
   - Influence the world and its factions through your choices.
   - Align with **Vengeance**, **Redemption**, or **Domination**.

6. **🏚️ Consequences & Rest**
   - Choices have lasting consequences on the world and its factions.
   - Rest in camps to recover health and resources.
   - The world changes based on your alignment and reputation.

## 🔮 **Magic System**

Magic is powerful but dangerous — overuse leads to corruption:

| Type | Effect | Risk | Alignment |
|-------|--------|------|-----------|
| **Fire** 🔥 | High damage + burning | Mana drain | Vengeance |
| **Water** 🌊 | Healing + crowd control | Slow cooldown | Redemption |
| **Earth** 🪨 | Defense + terrain manipulation | Heavy mana cost | Redemption |
| **Light** ☀️ | Buffs + purifying curses | Requires high mana | Redemption |
| **Darkness** ☠️ | Lifesteal + weakening enemies | Causes corruption | Domination |

👉 Overuse of dark magic increases Kaelen’s corruption — at high levels, it may take control.

---

## 🏹 **Combat System**

Strategic, terrain-based tactical combat:

✔️ **Positioning matters** – Use high ground and cover for advantage.
✔️ **Synergy of abilities** – Combine magic and physical attacks for devastating combos.
✔️ **Permanent injury** – Losing a unit may be permanent — except through dark resurrection.
✔️ **Weather and terrain effects** – Rain increases electrical damage, mud slows movement.

---

## 🏕️ **Company Management**

The Iron Crows are more than just soldiers — they are your family:

- **Recruit former comrades** – Some are lost, some broken — find them and rebuild the company.
- **Manage morale and loyalty** – A demoralized team will fight poorly — or turn on you.
- **Customize their gear and skills** – Adapt to the enemy and the environment.
- **Conflict and betrayal** – Some companions may have their own hidden agendas.

---

## 🔥 **Vengeance, Redemption, or Domination?**

Your choices shape Kaelen and the fate of Velsingrad:

### ⚔️ **Vengeance**

- Strike down the traitors.
- Sow chaos and fear.
- Build a legacy through blood.

### 🛡️ **Redemption**

- Save what remains of Velsingrad.
- Protect the innocent.
- Bring justice — not vengeance.

### 👑 **Domination**

- Embrace the dark power.
- Rule with strength and fear.
- Become a legend — or a monster.
